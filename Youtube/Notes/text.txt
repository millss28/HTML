line-height: adds space between lines

text-align: aligns text like google docs(left right center)

font-family: text font

font-size: size of font

font-style: change styles of text

font-weight: how bold font is

color: color of font

background-color: color of whole text box

margin: have to set margins to zero because paragraphs come with defaults,
        margin adds space outside of element(top,left,bottom,right)

width: the width determines what goes onto a new line, width of text box

cursor: adds cursor when mouse over text

text-decoration: additional style for text (underline)

<strong>= bold

<u>= underline

<span>= flexible
