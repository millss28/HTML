flexbox - flexible not as rigid as grid (headers, sidebars)

display: set to flex for flexbox

flex: take up remaining space relative to other elements (like fr)

flex-direction: sets the kind of layout (row, column)

flex-shrink: prevents a shrinking when closing window (0)

width: shrinks when closing window

justify-content: horizontal postioning of boxes (start,end,space-between)

align items: vertical positioning of boces (start end)

background-color: color of flexbox box

height: height of flexbox box

border-color: coloe of border
